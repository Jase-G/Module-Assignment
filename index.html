Imperial System Measurements:

Unit Definition Relativity to other units
Inch (in) Form of Imperial measurement (less than f) 1in = 2.54cm
Feet (f) Form of Imperial measurement (less than y. More than in) 1f = 12in (1f = 30.48cm)
Yard (y) Form of Imperial measurement (less than m. More than f) 1y = 3f (1y = 91.44cm)
Mile (m) Form of Imperial measurement (More than all Imperial measurements) (1m = 160,934cm)

Metric System Measurements

Unit Definition Relativity to other units
Milimeter (mm) Form of Metric measurement (less than c) 1mm = 0.0393701in
Centimeter (cm) Form of Metric measurement (less than d. More than mm) 1cm = 10mm (1cm = 0.393701in)
Decimeter (dm) Form of Metric measurement (less than M. More than cm) 1dm = 10cm (1dm = 3.93701in)
Meter (M) Form of Metric measurement (Less than de. More than dm) 1M = 10dm (1M = 39.3701in)
Decameter (de) Form of Metric measurement (less than he. More than M) 1de = 10M (1de = 393.701in)
Hectometer (he) Form of Metric measurement (less than km. More than de) 1he = 10de (1he = 3937.01in)
Kilometer (km) Form of Metric measurement (More than all Metric measurements) 1km = 10he (1km = 39370.08 inin)

Formulas

  • Slope(S) = Rise⁄Run
  • y-intercept = Y = M × X + B
  • Pythagorean Theorem = A2 + B2 = C2
  • Sine = Opposite⁄Hypotenuse
  • Cosine = Adjacent⁄Hypotenuse
  • Tangent = Opposite⁄Adjacent
